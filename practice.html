Study Tips

Practice


  1. Study vocabulary every day. Add images and color to the words that you don’t know.
  2. Make a list of words that have multiple meanings. They are tricky.
  3. Read news articles and add flashcards for words you don’t know. (Coming soon to Phonyc Frenzy)
  4. Know the format and timing of the test. See College Board’s website.
  5. Take at least one practice test before you take the real one. Review your practice test to see what you need to work on.
  6. Even if you are good at math, learn all the math terms in English.
