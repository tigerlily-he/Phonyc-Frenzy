Study Tips

De-Stress


  1. Take deep breaths by inhaling for four seconds and exhaling for four seconds.
  2. Close your eyes and imagine success. Positive thinking equals positive results.
  3. Remember that you are not alone. Think about all the ELL students who are also going through the same experience.
  4. Once the test is over, treat yourself.
  5. Reflect on your experience on our Facebook page. Or tweet us.
